school life

independence day celebration

We all gather in the PE field for the festivities. First we hoist the flag and sing the national anthem. Then there are a few performances like dances and songs. Lastly we sing the school song and are ready to go home.

Karnataka Rajothsava

We celebrate it in the ICSE auditorium.The principal, vice principal and chairpeople then light the lamp and after that are performances done by the students.We even hang flags and posters all around the school block.

Sports day

The Sports Day starts with an oath. Then there is the march with houses in order.After that are dances by all the grades and lastly, it ends with the sporting events like relay, hundred meter race and obstacle course.

What's New

  • New Indoor Gymnasium

    The magnificent and humongous new indoor sports complex houses basketball, badminton, volleyball, chess, table tennis, squash, simulated golf, swimming pool, snooker, yoga, dance, wrestling and several other sports facilities. We also proudly state that we have a shooting range that is modern, safe and provides amazing opportunities for the students to achieve their sporting dreams in non-mainstream sports and also teach life skills of concentration, patience and perfection

  • New Cafeteria

    To further improve the efficiency of the system, the cafeteria is a separate building with two floors to accommodate 900 children at a time. Kindergarten and primary level students are served on the ground floor. Middle and secondary level students partake in the buffet on the first floor.The cafeteria starts in the morning, serving breakfast to all the children, teachers and administrative staff. The premises are cleaned round the clock and maintained to the highest hygienic standards.
